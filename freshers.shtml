Freshers' information

If you're looking into coming to Trinity next year, we're looking forward to meeting you! Have a look below to see the sort of things we'll be running in Freshers' Week to help you settle into the family here, and keep an eye on this page for any updates.


We'll be running church search breakfasts on the first few Sundays of term, where you can join members of TCCU for breakfast and a chat before heading off to church. Members of TCCU go to a wide variety of churches, so you'll get an opportunity to try some different ones out, and if there's a church you'd like to try which none of us attend, we might be able to put you in touch with somebody to go with.

In Freshers' Week itself, we'll be running a social event, so you can get to know us, eat some food, and we'll also be able to help answer any questions you might have about TCCU, church in Cambridge, how to get settled in, or even the work here - that thing that you'll have to eventually get around to!

Members of TCCU are more than happy to meet up with you for a coffee (other beverages are available) throughout the year, if you just want to have a chat, or discuss something in particular. Have a chat with us at any of the events, or get in touch with our rep.
