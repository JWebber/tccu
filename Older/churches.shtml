Churches

TCCU is made up of people from a wide variety of church backgrounds (or even no church background), and, as such, there's no one church we all attend, or one way we always do things! If you're joining us in Cambridge this year, or on the lookout for a church to join, we should be able to help out.

Below is a list of churches either attended by members of TCCU, or known to be popular among students. It's by no means exhaustive, however, so be sure to do some of your own research!

St. Andrew the Great (StAG) is a lively, popular student church located in the centre of Cambridge, with services at 10:00, 11:30 (main student service) and 17:00 during term time.

Holy Trinity (HT) is another popular church amongst members of TCCU, with services at 09:30, 11:30 (main student service), and 17:30 during term time.

Eden Baptist Church is located on Fitzroy Street near the Grafton Centre, and also has a large student attendance, with its services on a Sunday at 10:00 and 18:00.

Fisher House (the University's Catholic Chaplaincy) is a five-minute walk from college and has services at 09:00 and 11:00 on Sundays.

This list obviously doesn't contain all of the churches in Cambridge, nor all of those with a sizeable student population! Other churches in the centre of town include St. Andrew's Street Baptist Church, the numerous other Anglican churches in the city (including St Bene't's, Great and Little St Mary's, and many more). Have a chat to any of us, or come along to church search breakfasts in the first few weeks of term, if you're unsure.
