Information

This page contains some (hopefully!) helpful information we've put together. We're hoping it's useful to anyone coming to Cambridge, or who wants to find out more about Christianity here.

Trinity CU is relatively diverse in church background, and so our members can advise on churches in Cambridge that suit whatever you're looking for! In the first few weeks of term, we run church search breakfasts, so you can accompany other students to some of the more popular churches in the city. There's a brief, and non-exhaustive, guide to some of the churches here on our website here.

Trinity is also lucky in having its own college chapel, with two chaplains available to all students in college. To find out more about services in the college chapel, and to get in touch with the chaplains, take a look at their website. The chaplains also run a wide array of events throughout term, all detailed online.

CICCU is the Cambridge Inter-Collegiate Christian Union, and represents a grouping of all of the individual CUs in all of the colleges. It runs its own Central events each week, alongside prayer meetings, and an annual Events Week. More information on everything CICCU does can be found on their website.

Each college has a rep, who's in charge of sorting the rest of us out (maybe...) Our rep for this year is Gui Freitas, a second-year English student. He's always available for a chat, and should be able to answer any questions you have about CU, or point you in the right direction for those answers!
